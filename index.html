Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

StreakMates ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: Display name, username, and email address (or Apple private relay email if you sign in with Apple).
  • Habit data: Habits you create, completion logs, streaks, and schedules.
  • Social data: Friend connections, shared habits, nudges, and messages you send or receive.
  • Device data: Push notification token for delivering reminders and social notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the StreakMates service.
  • To enable social features such as friend connections and habit sharing.
  • To send push notifications for habit reminders and friend activity.
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and push notifications. Privacy policy
  • Superwall: Subscription and paywall management. Privacy policy
  • Apple Sign In: Optional authentication via Apple ID.
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third-party services as described above, as required to operate the app.

Certain information (your display name, username, and shared habits) is visible to friends you connect with within the app.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings tab within the app. When you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, habits, and friend connections — is permanently removed.

To request data deletion without access to the app, email us at streakmatessupport@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

StreakMates is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
